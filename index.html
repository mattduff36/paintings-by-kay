Hi, I'm Kay

I started painting in 2013 after being inspired by watching Bob Ross. What began as a bit of fun quickly became a much-loved hobby and a way for me to relax.

I mostly paint nature scenes — trees, skies, water, seaside views — working from photographs that catch my eye with their beautiful colors. I mainly use acrylic paints (and sometimes oils), but acrylic is my favorite.

Painting is purely a passion for me, not a career. I occasionally accept commissions, although I prefer painting landscapes and scenery rather than people.

Thank you for visiting — I hope you enjoy my work as much as I enjoy creating it.
